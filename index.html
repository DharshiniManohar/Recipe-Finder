Best Recipes

CHOCOLATE CAKE

Mix flour, cocoa powder, sugar, eggs, and butter into a smooth batter. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 30-35 minutes. Let the cake cool, then frost with chocolate icing and serve.

BANANA MUFFINS

Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl; in another bowl, mix milk, eggs, and melted butter. Combine wet and dry ingredients, then fold in your choice of add-ins like berries or chocolate chips. Divide the batter into a muffin tin and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 18-20 minutes until golden brown.

BANANA BROWNIES

Mix mashed bananas with melted butter and sugar, then stir in flour, cocoa powder, and baking powder. Fold in chocolate chips, pour the batter into a pan, and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 20-25 minutes. Cool, then cut into squares.

HONEY-PANEER

Stir-fry paneer cubes in a hot pan until golden. In the same pan, add honey, soy sauce, and a splash of vinegar, then toss the paneer to coat evenly. Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions before serving.

CHICKEN BIRIYANI

Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices, then cook with sautéed onions. Layer partially cooked rice over the chicken, adding fried onions, mint, and coriander. Cover and simmer on low heat for 20-25 minutes, then serve.

PAN CAKES

Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl; in another bowl, whisk together milk, eggs, and melted butter. Combine the wet and dry ingredients until just blended, then cook on a hot griddle or pan until bubbles form on the surface, flip, and cook until golden brown.
