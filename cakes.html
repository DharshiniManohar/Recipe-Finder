CAKES

Chocolate Cake: Mix flour, cocoa powder, sugar, eggs, and butter into a smooth batter. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 30-35 minutes.

Vanilla Cake: Cream butter and sugar, then mix in eggs and vanilla. Add flour and milk, then bake at 350°F (175°C) for 25-30 minutes.

Red Velvet Cake: Combine flour, cocoa powder, sugar, buttermilk, and eggs. Add red food coloring, then bake at 350°F (175°C) for 30-35 minutes.

Butterscotch cake: Mix flour, cocoa powder, sugar, eggs, and butter into a smooth batter. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 30-35 minutes.

Honey cake: Cream butter and sugar, then mix in eggs and vanilla. Add flour and milk, then bake at 350°F (175°C) for 25-30 minutes.

Layer cake: Combine flour, cocoa powder, sugar, buttermilk, and eggs. Add red food coloring, then bake at 350°F (175°C) for 30-35 minutes.

Banana Muffins: Mix flour, cocoa powder, sugar, eggs, and butter into a smooth batter. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 30-35 minutes.

Roll Cake: Cream butter and sugar, then mix in eggs and vanilla. Add flour and milk, then bake at 350°F (175°C) for 25-30 minutes.

Black forest: Combine flour, cocoa powder, sugar, buttermilk, and eggs. Add red food coloring, then bake at 350°F (175°C) for 30-35 minutes.
