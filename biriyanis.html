Biriyani

Ambur Biriyani: Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices, then cook with sautéed onions until done. Layer partially cooked basmati rice over the chicken, adding fried onions, mint, and coriander. Cover and simmer on low heat for 20-25 minutes, then serve.

Malabar Biriyani: Marinate and cook chicken with yogurt, spices, onions, and tomatoes. Layer partially cooked basmati rice over the chicken with fried onions, mint, and coriander, then dum cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes.

Thalassery Biriyani: Marinate and cook chicken with yogurt, spices, onions, and tomatoes. Layer partially cooked basmati rice over the chicken with fried onions, mint, and coriander, then dum cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes.

Dindugal Biriyani: Marinate and cook chicken with yogurt, spices, onions, and tomatoes. Layer partially cooked basmati rice over the chicken with fried onions, mint, and coriander, then dum cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes.

Hyderabadi Biriyani: Marinate and cook chicken with yogurt, spices, onions, and tomatoes. Layer partially cooked basmati rice over the chicken with fried onions, mint, and coriander, then dum cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes.

Mutton Kemma Biriyani: Combine flour, cocoa powder, sugar, buttermilk, and eggs. Add red food coloring, then bake at 350°F (175°C) for 30-35 minutes.

Gilma Biriyani: Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices, then cook with sautéed onions until done. Layer partially cooked basmati rice over the chicken, adding fried onions, mint, and coriander. Cover and simmer on low heat for 20-25 minutes, then serve.

Mutton Dum Biriyani: Marinate and cook chicken with yogurt, spices, onions, and tomatoes. Layer partially cooked basmati rice over the chicken with fried onions, mint, and coriander, then dum cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes.

Fish Biriyani: Marinate and cook chicken with yogurt, spices, onions, and tomatoes. Layer partially cooked basmati rice over the fish with fried onions, mint, and coriander, then dum cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes.
